About Missions Central

Missions Central is the place for Auburn students to learn about and get involved in the world of Christian missions.

Our Purpose

The goal of this site is to promote and centralize knowledge about Christian Missions. By displaying information about different organizations and answering common questions, we aim to inform and inspire students to get involved in missions.

Our Goals

  • Promote awareness of the task of missions
  • Create encouragement for Christian circles engaging in missions
  • Promote knowledge of various missions organizations
  • Encourage christians to pray for missions
